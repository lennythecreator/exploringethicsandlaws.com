Olajumoke Oni is a desent of two Nigerian parents. She is a media professional representing women in sports. As a third year student of Multi-Platform Production at Morgan State University, Oni has emerged herself within her institution and has taken on various leadership roles. She is the Executive Producer of the Morgan Show which is the official sports show of her university, lead football manager for the players and coaches film, resident assistant and former access orientation peer mentor to the class of 2026. Oni says she makes efforts to be impactful in every single room she walks into. She looks forward to the world seeing her next moves, as it has only been the beginning for Olajumoke Oni.
